1. Discuss the scope of var, let, and const

Ans: var declarations are globally scoped or function scoped while let and const are block scoped. var variables can be updated and re-declared within its scope; let variables can be updated but not re-declared; const variables can neither be updated nor re-declared.But while var variables are initialized with undefined, let and const variables are not initialized. While var and let can be declared without being initialized,const must be initialized during declaration
